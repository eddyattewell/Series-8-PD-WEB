MDT Guide

MDT Main Menu

  • Civilian Search: Search by name or citizen identifier to retrieve the suspect’s information held by Series 8 PD.
  • Vehicle Search: Determine the owner of a vehicle and retrieve information available on the owner.
  • Incident Search: Search for specific incident numbers or ranges, filter by tags, and create incident reports.
  • Crime Management: Full list of crimes/charges held in MDT (use this if you can’t find a charge).

Civilian Profile

After finding a suspect in the MDT, you will have access to:

  • Profile Info: Civilian identifier, date of birth, nationality, gender.
  • Licenses: Driving licence and other licences; also shows driving points.
  • Markers: Active markers within the database.
  • BOLOs: Alerts the force that more information is required due to a prior situation.
  • Internal Notes: Notes from previous officers and a place to add new notes.
  • Digital References: View or add fingerprint/DNA references.
  • Aliases & Affiliations: Not used yet.
  • Photos: Civilian photos.
  • Vehicles: Full list of civilian vehicles.
  • Incidents: All incidents the civilian has been involved in.
  • Properties: Properties owned/linked.
  • Weapons: Registered weapons.

How to Structure a BOLO

Format: [Reason for the BOLO] [Incident reference number] [Date issued]

Example: [Conspiracy to Kidnap] [#999] [12/04/2023]

Detain and Arrest Markers

Use when the suspect has evaded police capture.

Format: [Reason for the Detain and Arrest] [Incident reference number] [Date issued]

Example: [Murder of multiple officers] [#999] [12/04/2023]

Organised Crime Markers

These markers indicate an individual has been part of organised crime activity. These are the responsibility of CID.

How to Get DNA & Fingerprints

DNA Reference

To obtain a civilian’s DNA, you need evidence bags. Once you have them, approach the suspect and use:

/take dna [id]

This provides an evidence bag containing the DNA reference. Add the reference to Digital References.

Fingerprint Reference

To obtain fingerprints, have the suspect place their finger on the fingerprint machine at Sinners or HQ. Access the machine, copy the reference number, then add it to Digital References.

Incident Creation

Title

The incident title must be filled in correctly:

[Incident number] - [Crime Committed] - [Date and Time]

Incident Write-up

Use the built-in template on incident creation. Click Insert Template (under the title), choose the correct template, then add your details. To add pictures or videos, click the media button and add the link.

Evidence

Physical evidence helps determine how a crime was carried out, link a suspect to the crime/identify the perpetrator, or exonerate an innocent person.

Common evidence types:

  • Blood
  • Casing
  • Weapon
  • Photo
  • Vehicle
  • Evidence Locker

Gather as much evidence as possible to aid CID investigations — the more evidence you have, the better the chance of solving the case.

Adding images to MDT: Take a screenshot of the evidence, save it to your PC, upload it into the evidence channel (Discord) using the bot, then right-click the image and select “copy link”.

Involvements

List all officers present at the scene, plus any members of the public who witnessed it. Add suspects so you can fill out the crimes committed section.

Regulation / Warning System (MDT Quality)

All MDT reports undergo quality testing by CID. Failure to meet MDT standards may result in a strike/warning.

  • Regulation 17: Verbal warning from Bronze or Silver+.
  • Regulation 18: Written warning and a meeting with SGT+ (goes on officer records).
  • Regulation 19: Command informs division command of officer incompetence and failure to follow SOP; dismissal from the police force.

This system starts for all officers at rank PC to allow time to learn the system.
