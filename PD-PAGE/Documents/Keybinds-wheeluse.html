Keybinds/Wheel Use

Handling

  • Left Arrow Key: Escort
  • Up Arrow Key: Cuff
  • Right Arrow Key: Place In/Out of Vehicle

Police Actions

  • Emergency Button: Panic! Use this if you are in danger and require immediate assistance.
  • GSR Test: Test the suspect’s hands for gunshot residue.
  • MDT: Opens up the MDT.
  • Community Service: Sends or releases suspect from community service.
  • Toggle Evidence: Get out your notepad and look for evidence. Evidence will show up on the floor.
  • Frisk: Pats down the suspect for any weapons (doesn’t show pockets).
  • Search: Searches the suspect (shows pockets).

More

  • Impound / Seize: Impounds the nearby vehicle.
  • Get Key: Get a key to an emergency vehicle.
  • Security Cams: Shows the security cameras in all stores and other locations.
  • Check VIN: Checks the VIN number of a vehicle.
  • Manage Licences: Manage civilian licences.
  • Check Status: NOT WORKING
  • BAC Test: NOT WORKING

More Continued

  • Put in Heli: Put a person in a helicopter.
  • Get in Heli: Get in the helicopter.
  • Take DNA Swab: Takes a person’s DNA.

Primary Radio Channels

  • Main: This is the main radio channel for Series 8 Police Department. Join this as soon as you clock on duty.
  • LHS: This is the main LHS radio channel. Join this in a medical emergency or when you are in need of LHS.
  • Fire: N/A at the moment.
  • Tac 1
  • Tac 2
  • Tac 3
  • Tac 4

Our TAC radio channels are used for isolated incidents where a number of officers will divert to a TAC radio (during a chase/raid/etc.). TAC channels are utilised to keep the main radio communications clear.

  • Joint: This radio can be utilised when on an incident with LHS or Joint Response.

Secondary Radio Channels

  • RPU
  • Response
  • CID
  • ARU
  • Gold Command

Traffic Management

  • Traffic Stop 1: Makes character wave away traffic.
  • Traffic Slow 2: Makes character wave away traffic.
  • Traffic Sign 1: Places traffic sign that stops local traffic.
  • Traffic Sign 2: Places traffic sign that slows local traffic.

Vehicle Wheel & Keybinds

  • Police Radar: TBA
  • Y: Opens the notification menu for calls (or check your settings).
  • G: Will set a waypoint to the selected call (must be in notification menu) (or check your settings).
  • Q: Will turn on emergency lights (or check your settings).
  • ALT: Will enable emergency sirens (must have lights on) (or check your settings).
