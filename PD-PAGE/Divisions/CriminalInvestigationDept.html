Criminal Investigation Dept.

Who are we?

The Criminal Investigation Department (CID) is a unit within the Series 8 Police Department composed of detectives charged with the duty of investigating major crimes such as robbery, firearms, narcotics, trafficking, organised, and violent crime.

Our main job is to conduct proactive and in-depth investigations into complex serious crimes, ensuring evidence is secured and cases are fully investigated, so a considered decision is taken about the prosecution of offenders.

Our Responsibilities

  • Investigate Organised Crime Groups
  • Investigate major crimes
  • Investigate high profile criminals
  • Maintain a high standard of discipline, duty, and professionalism
  • Crime scene investigations
  • Evidence collecting and processing

Transferring Units

  • Response to RPU: 7 days
  • Response / RPU to ARU: 14 days
  • Response / RPU / ARU to CID: head hunted
