Rules

  1. Always follow Chain Of Command. The chain of command is there and must be followed at all times, even if you believe your issue requires a certain rank then it will be escalated for you. e.g Bronze, Silver, Gold.
  2. Treat the members of the city with respect even if they're mouthing off you need to rise above it and ignore them always remember that if they're mouthing off in public hit them with fines for section 5 public order.
  3. You must obey the city laws and not break them while serving as a police officer this also includes corruption. If it is found out that you are you will be removed from the Series 8 Police Department and blacklisted.
  4. If you are pit trained Pitting in-city is allowed anything 80mph and under while out of city you may pit at 100mph when it is safe to do so.
  5. If you are chasing a suspect You have to wait a minute until you can taser them, however if they continue to run then you can tackle them to the ground as they are not a threat. Do Not Abuse your taser or it may get removed from yourself and a disciplinary may take place.
  6. From the moment you begin Processing a suspect , they become your responsibility and everything that happens to the Prisoner will fall on your shoulder. Do not leave them in the cells without telling an officer or a custody sergeant their charges.
  7. When arresting prisoners they need to get read the rights if it's unsafe to read them at the scene you must tell them before they walk through custody this includes charges caution and then custody rights.
  8. If you have an issue with another player be it from PD or another division, please speak with your Divisional Command and provide evidence via clips e.g Medal, OBS etc or whatever yo use to record your bodycam footage ( Record, Clip, Edit, Share, and Relive Your Game Clips & Gameplay - Medal.tv ) This is free to download and use.
  9. Officers must have bodycam footage available at all times. Upon this make sure that you use the in city bodycam so that the info is displayed in the top right corner otherwise it cant be used as evidence as well as using the in city camera to take evidence pics.
  10. When you get a vehicle from the garage you must check to see if you're allowed to use e.g only use an off road vehicle if you are off road trained.
  11. When you go on duty make sure you have your primary set to main and secondary set your division so that we can communicate with each other when needed so that we are not taking up main comms.
  12. Radio should be used only for communications like pursuits at no time should there be any chatting over radio with stuff that isn't important e.g ooc talk. If taking part in a pursuit move over to a different radio so that main comms are kept clear.
  13. No using dm's to discuss anything city related to anybody within the force keep it in the city if it is really important open a Silver/Gold Command Ticket then they will be happy to assist.
  14. While on duty you are expected to be professional at all times if you are seen mucking about you will be getting disciplinary action.
  15. If you are caught bad mouthing suspects or other officers this will not be tolerated and will lead to further punishment.
  16. On the MDT make sure that you are checking the Police Board for any information that you may need to know.
