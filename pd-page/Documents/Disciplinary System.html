Disciplinary System

Officers may be subject to a disciplinary hearing if they breach general conduct whilst on duty. This may also extend to off-duty officers if they cause fear amongst the public.

Regulations

  • Regulation 17: Marked verbal warning following the chain of command; you will be asked about the complaint and to give your version of events.
  • Regulation 18: Written notice on your record for 21 days if the statement is inadequate or guidelines were breached.
  • Regulation 18b: Demotion or removal from your current division and placement into another division depending on severity; may include suspension.
  • Regulation 19: Severe breach; terminated with immediate effect after an investigation.

Fines

  • Regulation 18 — £2000
  • Regulation 19 — £4000

You could be disciplined for: misconduct in public office, speaking down to the public/officers, unlawful use of equipment, breach of SOP, unprofessionalism (and other actions at command discretion).

Admin / Command only: Use the Warning/Strike Form to document warnings/strikes.
