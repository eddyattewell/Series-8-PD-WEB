Armed Response Unit

Who are we?

The Armed Response Unit (ARU) is a unit within the Series 8 Police Department who is widely regarded as one of the most skilled and well-equipped police firearms units in the world, and has been involved in a number of high-profile operations.

Is this for you?

ARU officers often operate in high-stress, rapidly changing situations where making the right decision quickly can mean the difference between life and death. The ability to make sound decisions under pressure is essential.

Clear, concise communication is critical to ensure a coordinated response to high-risk situations. ARU officers are held to a high standard of professionalism and discipline, and are accountable for all of their actions.

Expectation: If you cannot remain calm, professional, and communicative under pressure, ARU is not for you.

Our Responsibilities

  • Respond to high-risk situations and provide armed support to other police units as required.
  • Participate in training exercises and drills to maintain readiness for potential threats.
  • Comply with all relevant policies, procedures, and legislation governing firearms and tactical operations.
  • Maintain a high standard of professionalism, discipline, and accountability at all times.
  • Work closely with CID to bring down OCGs.

Transferring Units

  • Response to RPU: 7 days
  • Response to ARU: 14 days
  • Response to CID: head hunted
