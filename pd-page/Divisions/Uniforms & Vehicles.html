Uniforms & Vehicles

Uniform

One of the main and first things you will do as a police officer is get changed into the Series 8 Police uniform. Officers are expected to wear this at all times while on duty and must be wearing the correct uniform for their division/rank.

For a full list of your correct uniforms and vehicles, see your divisional information listed at the top of the site.

Vehicles

The Series 8 Police Department has vehicles ranging from day-to-day response vehicles to bikes and off-road vehicles, plus the police boat and NPAS.

Rule: You must only drive a vehicle that is assigned to your rank or division. The only time you may drive another vehicle is if Gold or Silver Command gives you permission for a specific situation. Once that situation is finished, you must return to your normal vehicle.

Weapons / Equipment

Weapons and police equipment are key tools for the Series 8 Police Department (from torches to tasers). You must only use weapons/equipment assigned to your rank/division. If anyone is caught using weapons/equipment they should not be, they will face a written warning or suspension.

Emergency Lighting System (ELS)

All fleet vehicles are equipped with an emergency lighting system (blue and white lights providing 360° visual awareness) and sirens to help officers respond quickly and safely while remaining visible and audible to the public.

ELS Keys

  • Lights — Q
  • Sirens — ALT
  • Siren Tone — R
  • Red Lights — K
  • Notice Board — L
